What to Do in an Emergency

  1. Stay Calm

    Take deep breaths and try to remain as calm as possible to think clearly.

  2. Assess the Situation

    Quickly determine the nature of the emergency and if anyone needs immediate medical attention.

  3. Call for Help

    Dial the appropriate emergency number for your situation. Remember to speak clearly.

  4. Provide Information

    Share your exact location, nature of emergency, and any other relevant details.

  5. Follow Instructions

    Listen carefully to the dispatcher and follow their guidance until help arrives.
