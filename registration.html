How We Protect Your Privacy

At Safe India, we prioritize your privacy above all else:

  • Your actual photos are never stored in our systems
  • We create secure, encrypted digital signatures from your images
  • All processing happens locally on your device
  • Only anonymous digital fingerprints are stored in our secure database
  • No humans ever see your uploaded photos during processing
  • You can delete your data from our systems at any time

If you have questions about our privacy measures, please contact our support team.
