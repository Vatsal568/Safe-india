Our Safety Features

Tools and resources designed to enhance your personal safety

Emergency Calling

Quick access to emergency services with one-tap calling functionality for immediate assistance in critical situations. The app offers voice-activated emergency calls for situations where touching the screen isn't possible.

Try Emergency Call Demo

Safety Map

Interactive map showing safety scores for different areas and locations of police stations, hospitals, and other emergency services. The map is regularly updated with crowdsourced safety data and official reports.

View Safety Map

Safety Alerts

Real-time alerts about safety concerns in your area including weather warnings, crime incidents, and traffic situations. You can customize alert types and notification preferences for your specific needs.

Configure Alerts

Safety Tips

Comprehensive guides and tips for staying safe in various situations across India. Our safety content is developed by experts and updated regularly to address emerging safety concerns.

Browse Safety Tips

Safety Circles

Create trusted networks of family and friends who can monitor your location and receive alerts if you need help. Safety circles allow for quick communication during emergencies.

Setup Safety Circle

Safe Route Planning

Find the safest routes between locations based on safety data, lighting conditions, and proximity to emergency services. The route planner factors in time of day and historical safety patterns.

Plan Safe Route

Multi-language Support

Access all safety information in multiple Indian languages to ensure critical safety information is accessible to everyone regardless of language preference.

Change Language

Offline Access

Critical safety information and emergency contacts are available offline, ensuring you can access important resources even without an internet connection.

Download Offline Data

SOS Emergency Button

Our most critical safety feature - the SOS Emergency Button provides immediate assistance when you need it most.

  • One-tap activation sends your location to emergency contacts
  • Automatic recording of surroundings for evidence
  • Connects to nearest emergency services
  • Works even with low battery and limited connectivity
